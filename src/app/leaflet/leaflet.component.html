This is mapping (GIS) demo Angular 18 app, based on the Leaflet.js and few plugins (terra-draw, leaflet-sidepanel). The fiction tech task was to display interactive map of particular Saudi Arabia region, and visualize future farming activities (like adding new "agro-circles"). For now the app is fully "frontendy": no servers or state preserving. All the provided "agro" data is completely random.

Although Saudi Arabia is widely thought of as a desert, it has regions suitable for agriculture. The major producing areas of Tabuk, Hail, and Qasim, average yields reached 8.1 tonnes per hectare (3.6 short ton/acre)
In 2018, Saudi Arabia produced:

  • 1,300,000 tons of date (2nd largest producer in the world);
  • 634,000 tons of watermelon;
  • 624,000 tons of barley;
  • 586,000 tons of wheat;
  • 482,000 tons of potato;
